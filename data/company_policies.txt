Enterprise Support & Corporate Policies

1. Travel Allowance & Reimbursement Policy
- All employee corporate business travel is eligible for a travel allowance.
- Standard daily meal allowance: employees are reimbursed up to a maximum of $150 per day for meals.
- Receipts are required for all individual meal charges exceeding $25.
- Alcohol charges are strictly non-reimbursable.
- Lodging guidelines: standard business hotel rates are covered up to $200 per night unless otherwise authorized.

2. Remote Work & Hybrid Schedule Policy
- All full-time employees are eligible for remote work subject to manager approval.
- Tenure requirement: a minimum of 6 months tenure with the company is required before entering a hybrid contract.
- Schedule structure: hybrid schedule of a minimum of 2 days onsite (in the local physical office) per week.
- Office hours: Core collaboration hours on onsite days are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

3. Computer & Equipment Provisioning Policy
- Standard issue hardware for all engineering, product, and operations staff is an Apple M3 MacBook Pro (16 GB unified memory, 512 GB SSD).
- Refresh cycle: all primary computing equipment is refreshed or upgraded every 3 years.
- Damaged items: replacement of damaged or lost equipment requires a signed IT incident report form and manager approval.
- Ergonomic accessories: employees can request up to $250 for home office ergonomic accessories (e.g. keyboard, chair cushion) with proof of purchase.
